Activities

Take a break from life with these activities

Go on a run or take a walk outside

Walking and running are proven to boost your mood and help you better focus. By moving outside you will see your stress melt away and you will be astonished by the breathtaking nature.

Social Media Detox Day

Social media can cause unnecessary stress. Improve your overall mood. If you've been feeling stressed out or depressed, this is a good time to take a social media detox.

Arts and Crafts

Get wild and creative by doing some arts and crafts! You can use art as a creative outlet to express your thoughts and feelings. After all the best artwork is the ones that come from the heart!

Get off your phone.

Overchecking your email, watching irrelevant news, and excess screen time is proven to make you unhappy. If it's important, trust that you will hear about it anyway. Take time instead to spend time with others or enjoy nature.
